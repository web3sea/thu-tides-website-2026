# Thu Tides — Product Roadmap

This roadmap breaks the Thu Tides landing page into 5 development sections. Each section is self-contained and can be designed and built independently.

---

## Section 1: Hero

**Title:** Hero / Introduction

**Description:** The opening section that immediately captures attention and communicates what Thu Tides offers. Features a stunning hero image showcasing coastal/diving photography, headline messaging about creative collaboration for hospitality brands, and primary call-to-action to explore collaboration opportunities.

**Key Elements:**
- Hero image/video background
- Main headline and tagline
- Primary CTA button
- Brief value proposition
- Visual impact that sets the tone

---

## Section 2: Services

**Title:** What We Offer

**Description:** Detailed overview of the creative services Thu Tides provides to hospitality partners. Covers photography and video content creation, Instagram promotion and reach, and the value of authentic visual storytelling for coastal brands.

**Key Elements:**
- Photography services
- Video content creation
- Instagram promotion and audience reach
- Content strategy and artistic approach
- Benefits for hospitality brands

---

## Section 3: Portfolio

**Title:** Our Work

**Description:** Visual gallery showcasing previous collaborations with boutique hotels, homestays, dive resorts, and liveaboards. Organized to highlight different property types and locations, demonstrating the range and quality of Thu Tides' work.

**Key Elements:**
- Image/video gallery
- Project thumbnails with hover effects
- Filter by property type or location
- Case study highlights
- Before/after or impact stories (optional)

---

## Section 4: About

**Title:** About Thu Tides

**Description:** The story behind Thu Tides, the approach to creative collaboration, and the vision for promoting travel through artistic expression. Establishes credibility and connects with the target audience on values and philosophy.

**Key Elements:**
- Thu Tides story and background
- Creative philosophy and approach
- Team/founder introduction
- Instagram community highlight
- Why we focus on coastal hospitality

---

## Section 5: Collaboration

**Title:** Let's Collaborate

**Description:** Clear explanation of partnership models (barter and paid options), how the collaboration process works, and an easy contact form for brands to reach out. Removes friction and makes it simple to start a conversation.

**Key Elements:**
- Partnership models explained (barter & paid)
- Collaboration process overview
- Contact/inquiry form
- Social media links
- FAQ or common questions (optional)

---

## Implementation Order

1. **Hero** — Sets the stage, can be built first
2. **Services** — Core offering, high priority
3. **Portfolio** — Requires content, can build structure early
4. **About** — Builds trust, medium priority
5. **Collaboration** — Conversion point, critical for CTA

---

## Notes

- Each section is designed to work independently
- Sections can be reordered on the page as needed
- All sections should be mobile-responsive
- Consistent design language across all sections
- Smooth scrolling and transitions between sections
